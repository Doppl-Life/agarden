## Growth — Problem recovery
### Surface complaint
Paparazzi drones can photograph open decks, ruining privacy.
### Deleted assumption
Privacy failure is solely due to the *capture* of an image.
### Hidden variable
The legal and technical feasibility of rendering captured data useless at the point of transmission/storage.
### Actual problem
Intercepting or corrupting the high-bandwidth, non-RF data stream (e.g., optical links, memory cards) *after* capture but *before* exfiltration into a usable format.
### Candidate response
Establish an automated 'Data Fog' system using multiple synchronized, low-power visual decoys and localized electromagnetic field emitters that do not jam RF signals but induce temporary, highly specific sensor saturation or corruption in the drone camera lens elements. These fields are tuned to introduce complex optical noise patterns (e.g., patterned chromatic aberration) directly onto the image sensor plane of the passing drone, resulting in unusable metadata-rich garbage files.

- Drone Signature Analysis (High frequency)
