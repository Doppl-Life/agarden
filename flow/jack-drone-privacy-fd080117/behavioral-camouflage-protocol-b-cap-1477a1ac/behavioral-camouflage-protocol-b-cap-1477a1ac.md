## Growth — Problem recovery
### Surface complaint
Guests are visible on open decks, creating scandalous photographs.
### Deleted assumption
That physical security measures can solve a psychological/behavioral problem.
### Hidden variable
The paparazzi's expectation of predictable human behavior in an isolated luxury setting.
### Actual problem
Implementing dynamic, low-visibility environmental modifications and scheduled 'distractions' that make the yacht appear non-stationary or temporarily uninteresting to external observers without disrupting the internal party atmosphere.
### Candidate response
Institute a rotating schedule of highly visible but meaningless activity (e.g., coordinated group movement into specialized, shaded zones; temporary deployment of large, benign physical props/art installations) that changes the yacht's visual profile every 30 minutes. This constant, unpredictable 'shifting pattern' makes it impossible for the drone operator to establish a reliable subject focus or predict where the action will be next, leading to wasted footage.
