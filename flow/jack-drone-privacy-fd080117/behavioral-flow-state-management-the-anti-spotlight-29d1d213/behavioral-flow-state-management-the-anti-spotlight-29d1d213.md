## Growth — Problem recovery
### Surface complaint
Paparazzi drones can photograph open decks, ruining privacy.
### Deleted assumption
Privacy requires physical separation from public view.
### Hidden variable
The psychological susceptibility of high-profile individuals to the *perception* of observation.
### Actual problem
Managing the internal and external perception of being constantly watched by subtly redirecting attention vectors away from predictable, valuable 'photo zones.'
### Candidate response
Implement a synchronized, ambient environmental management system that continuously modifies the yacht's activity patterns and lighting to create a state of perpetual, low-level cognitive distraction. This involves orchestrating non-repeating, unexpected events (e.g., spontaneous, localized music changes; shifting dining areas; deploying varied scents) designed to keep guests in an elevated 'flow state,' reducing their awareness of external surveillance or the *value* they place on any single visible location.
