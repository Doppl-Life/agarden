## Growth — Problem recovery
### Surface complaint
Paparazzi drones photograph open decks before anyone can react.
### Deleted assumption
That privacy must be maintained in the physical space of the yacht's deck.
### Hidden variable
The ability to separate the *experience* of the party from its physical location.
### Actual problem
Creating a temporary, localized 'sensory bubble' that makes the internal environment indistinguishable from an artificially controlled setting (like a film set), thus invalidating external visual data.
### Candidate response
Use advanced, non-visible atmospheric and acoustic dampening systems combined with hyper-realistic, full-spectrum projection mapping to make the yacht’s interior appear perpetually enclosed within a stable, engineered environment. This means that even if filmed externally, the camera feed would capture only distorted reflections of an artificial 'set' rather than the genuine, variable reality of people interacting in open space.
### Skin in the Game
- Virtual Reality/Augmented Reality projection mapping
- Acoustic dampening for echo cancellation
- Advanced material science (light absorption)
### Sprouts
- Film set construction simulating natural environments
- Anechoic chambers for recording sensitive audio
- Habitat simulation in deep-sea research
