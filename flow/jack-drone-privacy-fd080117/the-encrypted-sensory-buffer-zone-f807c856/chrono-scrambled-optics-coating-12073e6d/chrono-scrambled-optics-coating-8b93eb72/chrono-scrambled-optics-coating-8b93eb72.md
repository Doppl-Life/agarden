# Chrono-Scrambled Optics Coating

prev_id: [[chrono-scrambled-optics-coating-12073e6d]]

## Context

Reseeded from a doppl leaf — the unlock becomes the seed of a new island.

## Synopsis

Applying ultra-thin, programmable metamaterial coatings to key visible surfaces (balcony railings, pillars) that actively randomize light reflection across specific temporal intervals (milliseconds), causing rapid 'flicker ghosting' when filmed by persistent optical recording devices.
