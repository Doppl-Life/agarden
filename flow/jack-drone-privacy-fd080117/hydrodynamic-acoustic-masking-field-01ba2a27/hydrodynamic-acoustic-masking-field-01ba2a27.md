## Growth — Problem recovery
### Surface complaint
Paparazzi drones can photograph open decks, ruining privacy.
### Deleted assumption
Silence and tranquility are necessary for effective privacy.
### Hidden variable
The ability of complex acoustic noise to mask the *sound* of activity or movement that signals a valuable target (the 'presence' signature).
### Actual problem
Maintaining an ambient, natural level of background acoustic complexity (white/pink noise) that effectively masks subtle sounds associated with high-value activities (e.g., hushed conversation, specific movements, laughter), making the yacht appear acoustically indistinguishable from general maritime chaos.
### Candidate response
Deploy a network of ultra-low frequency, distributed acoustic emitters hidden within the water and structure. These emitters generate complex, pseudo-random 'masking noise' that mimics natural coastal sounds (e.g., distant wave breaking, passing boat engines, wildlife calls) but at a higher, more unpredictable density than ambient conditions. This makes it impossible for an external listening device to isolate specific conversations or actions as distinct from the background.
