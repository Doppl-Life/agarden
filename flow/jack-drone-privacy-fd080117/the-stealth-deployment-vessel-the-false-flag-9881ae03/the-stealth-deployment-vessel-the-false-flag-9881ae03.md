## Growth — Problem recovery
### Surface complaint
Paparazzi drones photograph open decks before anyone can react.
### Deleted assumption
That the yacht must defend itself from external threats coming from a specific direction (the shore/paparazzi).
### Hidden variable
The operational visibility and tracking capabilities of non-private vessels in busy waters.
### Actual problem
Introducing an artificial, highly visible 'distraction' asset into the immediate vicinity that is *more* interesting or legally demanding to track than the yacht itself, diverting attention and resources.
### Candidate response
Pre-position a secondary, non-ostentatious support vessel (e.g., labeled as a research survey boat, emergency medical unit, or private academic filming platform) in the operational area. This 'False Flag' asset is programmed to generate necessary noise/activity that forces surveillance drones and paparazzi vessels to focus their attention on its unique activity or perceived threat level, effectively overshadowing the yacht’s presence.
### Skin in the Game
- Maritime SAR (Search and Rescue) protocols
- Survey mapping technology
- Logistics of controlled 'misdirection'
### Sprouts
- Military deception operations (dummy assets)
- Controlled environmental testing sites
- Operational masking in high-security zones
