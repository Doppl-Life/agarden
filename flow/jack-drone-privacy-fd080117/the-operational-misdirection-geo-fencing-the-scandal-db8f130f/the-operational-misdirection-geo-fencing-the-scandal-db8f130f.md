## Growth — Problem recovery
### Surface complaint
Paparazzi drones photograph open decks before anyone can react.
### Deleted assumption
That the yacht must remain in a single, fixed location for privacy to be maintained.
### Hidden variable
The legal and logistical vulnerability of operating in specific, high-profile zones.
### Actual problem
Creating an operational profile that is legally ambiguous or logistically difficult for non-authorized commercial surveillance assets (drones/vessels) to track or operate within.
### Candidate response
Establish a pre-approved, highly mobile 'operational zone' far from typical tourist paths and in waters requiring specialized permits (e.g., adjacent to military training areas or scientific monitoring zones). The yacht must maintain constant movement patterns that are difficult to predict and require continuous re-permitting/reporting, forcing surveillance assets into legal gray zones.
### Skin in the Game
- Maritime law and jurisdictional mapping
- Advanced predictive modeling of maritime traffic
- High-frequency operational agility training (crew)
### Sprouts
- Disaster zone triage/response protocols
- Deep-sea scientific research vessel deployment
- Autonomous, shifting commercial pipelines
