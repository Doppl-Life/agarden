## Growth — Problem recovery

### Surface complaint

Investors focus on ride-hailing unit economics and driver displacement.

### Deleted assumption

Insurance-linked securities and reinsurance pools price risk based on stable human-error frequency distributions.

### Hidden variable

Crash volume acts as the primary trigger and liquidity engine for catastrophe bonds, specialty auto captives, and secondary liability markets.

### Actual problem

Capital markets face basis risk when autonomy decouples vehicle value from traditional Poisson loss triggers, causing sudden repricing events and liquidity crunches in fleet risk transfer.

### Candidate response

Transition catastrophe modeling to AI-fleet stress testing with real-time telemetry triggers and establish a sovereign backstop for autonomy-specific tail risk.
