# Preventative Liability Forward Markets

prev_id: [[warranty-cds-standardization-3b86cbfe]]
## Trace
### Case study · synopsis

Mandating a 24-month liability transfer window forces OEMs, fleet operators, and insurers to structurally align warranty terms, insurance deductibles, and operational SLAs around rapid indemnification triggers rather than static depreciation schedules.

### Problem recovery · synopsis

Unhedged tail risk in early-life fleet deployments creates capital inefficiency and pricing opacity.

### Claim
Invert the indemnification flow by requiring OEMs to pay upfront for 24-month liability acceptance, secured not by cash reserves but by predictive maintenance algorithms and real-time fleet health data.
