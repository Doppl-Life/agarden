## Growth — Problem recovery
### Surface complaint
Indemnification claims linger beyond useful asset life, obscuring true early-window performance metrics.
### Deleted assumption
Liability transfer must be permanent or open-ended once a defect is discovered, regardless of claim status.
### Hidden variable
Long-tail claims distort accounting and delay capital recovery for the actual 24-month risk curve.
### Actual problem
Accounting complexity and open-ended liability provisions prevent accurate pricing and provisioning of the true 24-month risk exposure.
### Candidate response
Implement hard sunset clauses where all indemnification rights expire at month 24 regardless of claim status, forcing upfront financial provisioning for the exact risk window.
