# Directed Atmospheric Particle Veil

prev_id: [[directed-atmospheric-particle-veil-a68efb08]]

## Context

Reseeded from a doppl leaf — the unlock becomes the seed of a new island.

## Synopsis

Generating a localized plume of non-toxic, micro-particulate matter (e.g., tailored bio-luminescent dust or colloidal silica) at the air-yacht boundary layer that is invisible to the naked eye but achieves two things: 1) It destabilizes airborne photo/video capture; and 2) It creates a randomized diffraction pattern specifically designed to corrupt geometric depth mapping for drones.
