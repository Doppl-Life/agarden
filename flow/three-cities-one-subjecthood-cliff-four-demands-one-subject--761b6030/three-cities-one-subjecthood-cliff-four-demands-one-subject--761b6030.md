### Recovered problem

Invert the traffic-stop story toward the municipalities whose general-fund math breaks when the citeable driver vanishes—not the fleet operator who replaces them. The child imports the secondary constraint from Four Demands, One Subject-Transfer Fork.

### Hidden constraint

Phoenix (court/traffic-fine trust + transportation GO exposure), Austin (municipal court operations tied to moving-violation volume), and San Francisco (traffic-enforcement receipts embedded in general-fund filler) each lose a load-bearing fine line once AV corridors remove the human subject. Stop-volume decline does not hit bond covenants immediately: appropriation pressure shows in 2–4 quarters as citation receipts miss budget, then rating-review lag of 12–24 months converts the miss into covenant language. Subjecthood removal is the causal hinge—without it this is ordinary municipal finance noise. It is tempered by Demands: (1) municipal revenue from citeable individuals, (2) investigatory leverage at roadside contact, (3) safety signaling via visible sanction, (4) officer labor utilization on high-yield stops. Incentives: fine probability × enforceability × collection friction. Feedback: citation volume → actuarial frequency → bond/budget flows. Subject-transfer fork: fleet operator becomes operator-of-record while demands 2–4 partially persist in new forms. Falsifier: if >55% of moving-violation citations in AV-pilot corridors still name a human occupant after 24 months, fleet operators have absorbed subjecthood cosmetically and the old pipeline persists.

### Falsifier

Recovers the problem as a timed fiscal cliff keyed to citeable-subject disappearance, not a generic autonomy revenue debate. + Mutates the survivor by making the fork falsifiable and keeping the frame sharper than a generic incentives essay.

#### Cost-efficiency +2

Testing the claim requires assembling municipal budget lines, citation records, and bond covenants across three jurisdictions—tractable with public data but not a cheap single-source check.

#### Relevance +4
