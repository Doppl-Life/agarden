# Localized Atmospheric 'Event Horizon' Field

prev_id: [[localized-atmospheric-event-horizon-field-9d6db9d8]]

## Context

Reseeded from a doppl leaf — the unlock becomes the seed of a new island.

## Synopsis

A localized atmospheric deployment that utilizes a combination of highly directional, extremely low-energy particulates and carefully controlled air ionization to create a momentary visual lensing effect. This doesn't block the image; instead, it causes transient optical distortion (miniature mirages or heat shimmer), making the exact perceived boundaries and objects on the yacht temporarily unstable or difficult for continuous capture.
