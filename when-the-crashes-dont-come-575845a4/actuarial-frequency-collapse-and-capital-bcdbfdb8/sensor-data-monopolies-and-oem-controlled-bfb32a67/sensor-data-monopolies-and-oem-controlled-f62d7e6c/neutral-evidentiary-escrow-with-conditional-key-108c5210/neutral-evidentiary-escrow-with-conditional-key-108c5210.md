## Growth — Problem recovery
### Surface complaint
Autonomous sensor networks enable manufacturers to replace litigation with controlled liability arbitration and rent extraction.
### Deleted assumption
Vehicle manufacturers must retain continuous custody of operational data for warranty and safety compliance.
### Hidden variable
Custodial separation between data ownership and legal access dictates bargaining leverage in incident resolution.
### Actual problem
Integrated data custody and ownership creates structural rent-seeking by allowing OEMs to gatekeep the sole viable evidence path for liability claims.
### Candidate response
Legally mandate a tripartite evidentiary escrow system where real-time sensor streams are encrypted and held by a neutral digital custodian, with decryption keys released only upon verified legal/arbitral request.

- consumer litigation funds
