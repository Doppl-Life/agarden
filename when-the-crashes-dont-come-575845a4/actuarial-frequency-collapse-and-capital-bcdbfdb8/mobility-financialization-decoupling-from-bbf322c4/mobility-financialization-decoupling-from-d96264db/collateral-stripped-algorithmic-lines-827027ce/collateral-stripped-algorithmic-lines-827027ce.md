## Growth — Problem recovery
### Surface complaint
Lenders refuse to finance mobility services without traditional credit checks.
### Deleted assumption
Physical asset ownership is necessary to secure unsecured usage contracts.
### Hidden variable
Collateral dependency locks capital into static balance sheets instead of dynamic cash flows.
### Actual problem
Balance sheet rigidity prevents financial institutions from capturing high-frequency mobility revenue streams.
### Candidate response
Strip collateral requirements entirely and replace with algorithmic solvency scoring driven by real-time usage APIs.
